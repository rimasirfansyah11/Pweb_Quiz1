LOCAL FOOD

Palembang's finest local food is "Pempek".

This food is mainly made of fish, flour, salt, and other tasty ingredients. You may eat this food along with it's special sauce caled "Cuko" which is made with brown sugar, sugar, salt, chilly.

The food taste so good. With the fish as ingredients, it may be a little smelly, but don't let it fool you for the food itself tastes great. It is crispy outside, but squishy inside and also have so many different stuffing.
